What is the GIFT Platform?

The GIFT Platform is a set of tools, including this app, that lets you send and receive 'gifts'.
It is a service provided by the EU-funded GIFT project, which is investigating ways to combine digital content with real cultural artifacts - museum exhibits, historical sites, and so on - to provide new ways for visitors to engage with heritage.

What is this app?

This app lets you send a 'gift' to another user. Gifts in the GIFT Platform are packages of digital content that you create for another user to experience when they are in a particular situation, e.g. near a specific cultural artifact.
Using the GIFT Wrapper app you set up that situation, create the digital gift, and send it to the lucky recipient.
